## Notes that apply to every client

- Rate limit: 60 calls per 15 minutes per IP.
- Transport: streamable HTTP, JSON-RPC 2.0. Content-type `application/json`. Accept `application/json, text/event-stream`.
- Older clients that expect stdio use the [`mcp-remote`](https://www.npmjs.com/package/mcp-remote) bridge. Each guide shows the command.
- Prefer a guided UI? The wizard at [ccpedia.xyz/for-ai](https://ccpedia.xyz/for-ai) auto-detects your client and pings the server live.
